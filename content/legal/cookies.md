# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-26

This Cookie Policy explains how footballevents.eu, operated by [Company OÜ, Tallinn, Estonia], uses cookies and similar technologies. It should be read together with our [Privacy Policy](./privacy.md). Contact: **legal@footballevents.eu**.

## 1. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your browser. "Similar technologies" include localStorage, sessionStorage, and pixels. Cookies can be:

- **Session cookies** — deleted when you close your browser.
- **Persistent cookies** — kept for a defined time.
- **First-party** — set by us.
- **Third-party** — set by other domains loaded inside our pages.

## 2. Our Approach

We try to keep cookies to a minimum. We do **not** use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, or behavioral profiling. The only cookies we set ourselves are strictly necessary for the Service to work.

Because we do not run any non-essential first-party tracking, you will not see an intrusive consent banner asking you to accept marketing cookies. We do, however, provide a clear notice and respect your "Do Not Track" preferences where technically possible.

## 3. Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Strictly Necessary (First-Party)

These cookies are required for core functionality and cannot be disabled without breaking the Service.

| Name | Purpose | Type | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| `session` (or equivalent auth cookie) | Keeps you signed in | HTTP-only, Secure | Up to 30 days |
| `csrf_token` | Protects forms against cross-site request forgery | HTTP-only, Secure | Session |
| `locale` | Remembers your language choice (EN, RU, DE, ES) | First-party | 1 year |
| `theme` / UI prefs | Remembers minor interface preferences | First-party | 1 year |

Legal basis: **strictly necessary** under the ePrivacy Directive — no consent required.

### 3.2 Analytics (Cookie-less)

We use **Plausible Analytics** to measure aggregate usage (page views, referrers, country). Plausible is **cookie-less** and does not store any persistent identifier on your device. It does not collect personal data and is GDPR/PECR-friendly. No consent banner is required for Plausible under current EU guidance.

### 3.3 Third-Party Cookies (Loaded On Demand)

Third-party cookies are only set when you actively use the related feature.

| Provider | When loaded | Purpose | More info |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Stripe** | On checkout pages and when entering payment details | Fraud prevention, card payments | stripe.com/privacy |
| **YouTube** (privacy-enhanced mode where possible) | When an embedded YouTube video is rendered on a listing page | Video playback, abuse prevention | policies.google.com |
| **Vimeo** | When an embedded Vimeo video is rendered on a listing page | Video playback | vimeo.com/privacy |

These providers may set their own cookies under their own privacy policies. We do not control those cookies. Where feasible, video embeds use privacy-enhanced or "do-not-track" modes and load only after you interact with the player or the page.

We do **not** load Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, TikTok Pixel, or similar advertising trackers.

## 4. Local Storage

We may use the browser's `localStorage` to store small UI state (e.g., dismissed banners, draft form input, saved-event IDs for guests). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us unless required for a feature you actively use.

## 5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies in your browser settings:

- **Chrome** — Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data.
- **Firefox** — Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
- **Safari** — Settings → Privacy.
- **Edge** — Settings → Cookies and site permissions.

You can also:

- delete cookies already stored,
- block third-party cookies,
- enable "Do Not Track" or Global Privacy Control signals.

Blocking strictly necessary cookies will prevent you from signing in or submitting forms. Blocking third-party cookies may break embedded videos or payment flows.

## 6. Mobile and Similar
