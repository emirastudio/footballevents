# Refund Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-27

This Refund Policy explains the rules that apply to paid services on footballevents.eu, operated by [Company OÜ, Tallinn, Estonia] ("we", "us"). It supplements our Terms of Service. All amounts are in EUR. Contact: **legal@footballevents.eu**.

## 1. No-Refund Policy

**All payments to footballevents.eu are final and non-refundable.** This applies in particular to:

- **Organizer subscriptions** (monthly and annual plans),
- **Boosts and featured placements** (one-time purchases),
- **Advertising placements** (banners, sponsored categories, newsletter sponsorship),
- **Any other paid feature** offered now or in the future.

By purchasing a paid feature you acknowledge and agree that no refund will be issued — in whole or in part — under any circumstances except as strictly required by mandatory law.

## 2. Scope

This policy covers payments made directly to us. It does **not** cover payments made by participants directly to organizers (participation fees, deposits, travel, equipment, etc.). Those are governed by the organizer's own terms — please contact the relevant organizer for any questions about money paid to them.

## 3. Subscriptions

Subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each billing period.

- **Cancellation** — you can cancel at any time from your dashboard. Cancellation stops the **next** renewal. Your subscription remains active until the end of the period already paid for.
- **No mid-cycle refunds.** Cancelling, downgrading, ceasing to use the Service, account suspension, or termination for breach do **not** entitle you to a refund of any portion of the current period.
- **Annual plans** are paid up-front and are non-refundable after activation.
- **Price changes.** If we increase prices, we give advance notice. You may cancel before the new price takes effect; the new price applies at the next renewal otherwise.

## 4. Boosts, Featured Placements and Advertising

Boosts, featured placements and advertising are one-time purchases that increase visibility for a defined period. They are **non-refundable** in all cases, including (without limitation):

- the listing was removed for breach of our Terms,
- the boost overlapped with an outage or technical issue on our side,
- the campaign produced fewer impressions, clicks or conversions than expected.

We do not guarantee any specific level of visibility, traffic, leads, sales or applications.

## 5. B2B Service — No Statutory Withdrawal

Paid features are offered to organizers and businesses acting in a professional (B2B) capacity. The 14-day right of withdrawal under the EU Consumer Rights Directive does **not** apply to B2B purchases and is therefore not available for these services. Where, despite this, the Service is purchased by a consumer, by activating any paid feature you expressly request immediate performance and acknowledge that any statutory right of withdrawal is lost as soon as the service has been performed.

## 6. Chargebacks

Please contact us before initiating a chargeback — most issues are resolved faster by email. Filing a chargeback for a payment that is non-refundable under this policy may result in:

- immediate suspension or termination of the account,
- recovery of the disputed amount plus chargeback and processing fees,
- referral to debt collection,
- a permanent ban from creating future accounts.

If a chargeback is filed and later resolved in our favor, the related service is not reinstated automatically.

## 7. Failed Payments

If a renewal payment fails, we retry for a limited period and notify you by email. If payment cannot be collected, paid features are paused until payment succeeds. Reactivation does not restore visibility lost during the paused period.

## 8. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will reflect the most recent revision. Material changes will be announced by email or in-app notice.
